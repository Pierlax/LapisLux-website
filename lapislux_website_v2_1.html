The Challenge

The Impossible Choice

Superyacht designers have always faced an impossible trade-off: heavy natural stone that compromises stability and fuel efficiency, or lightweight artificial alternatives that lack the depth and authenticity of real stone.

LAPISLUX eliminates this compromise.

Our Story

From the remote mines of Helmand to the artisan workshops of Carrara. We transform the heaviest element on earth into light.

Every LAPISLUX panel is a masterpiece of engineering and artistry: 70% lighter than solid stone, IMO/MED certified for marine application, and crafted with Italian precision.
